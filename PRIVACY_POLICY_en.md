Hongssine ("the Developer") respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected in the mobile game application **JIA BLOCK** ("the App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1-1. Information the Developer does NOT collect
The App has no sign-up feature. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.

### 1-2. Information stored only on your device
Game scores, best records, stage progress, coins, and sound/haptic settings are stored **only on your device (local storage)** and are never sent to the Developer's servers. They are removed when you delete the App.

### 1-3. Information collected by third parties (ad provider)
The App uses **Google AdMob** to display ads. To serve and measure ads, Google and its advertising partners may collect and process:

- Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA / device advertising ID)
- Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
- Approximate location (IP-based, regional level)
- App usage and ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is handled under Google's privacy policy.
▸ Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
▸ How Google uses information from ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## 2. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency, the App asks for your **consent before tracking** your activity across other companies' apps and websites.
- You may decline. **Ads will still be shown** even if you decline (they may be non-personalized).
- You can change this anytime in **Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Tracking**.

## 3. How Information Is Used
- Serving and measuring in-app advertisements
- Maintaining and improving service quality
- Preventing abuse (e.g., invalid traffic)

## 4. Sharing / Processing by Third Parties
The Developer does not sell your personal information. Data is processed via **Google AdMob** for advertising, subject to Google's policies.

## 5. Data Retention & Security
- Game data is stored only on your device and removed when the App is deleted.
- Retention and security of advertising data follow Google's policies.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The App is not specifically directed to children under 14 (under 13 in the U.S.) and does not knowingly collect their personal information. If we learn such data was provided, we will promptly delete it.

## 7. Your Rights
- **Opt out of personalized ads:** decline tracking or reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.
  - iOS: Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Tracking / Apple Advertising
- **Delete local data:** deleting the App removes all game data stored on your device.
